## Having a Conversation

### Voice Input (Recommended)
1. **Click and hold** the microphone button to start recording
2. **Speak in Mandarin** - the app will transcribe your speech
3. **Release the button** to stop and send your message

The AI will respond naturally, keeping the conversation flowing!

### Text Input (Alternative)
If you prefer typing or speech recognition isn't working, you can type your message directly in the text box.

**Pro Tip**: The AI responds with audio by default - you'll hear the pronunciation naturally.

## Troubleshooting

### Speech Recognition Not Working?
- Make sure you're using **Chrome browser** (best compatibility)
- Check that your microphone permissions are enabled
- Try speaking more clearly and at a moderate pace
- Use the text input as an alternative

### Can't See Pinyin or Translations?
- Check your settings on the setup page
- Make sure "Show Pinyin" or "Show English Translations" is enabled

### Need Help?
- Use the Q&A panel to ask questions about your conversation
- Share feedback via the "Share Feedback" button in the sidebar
